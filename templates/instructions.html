How to Use the Remove Duplicates Tool

1. Upload a CSV File

Click on the "Choose File" button, select your CSV file, and then click "Upload and Process." The system will automatically remove duplicates and provide you with a deduplicated file to download.

2. Paste CSV Data Directly

Paste your CSV data into the text box provided under "Paste Text to Remove Duplicates" and click "Process Text." You will see the deduplicated data on the next page, with an option to download it as a CSV file.
